Cart

How can I return a product?

You can return a product by contacting our customer service within 30 days of purchase.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we offer international shipping to select countries. Please check our shipping policy for more details.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards, PayPal, and Apple Pay.

How long does shipping take?

Shipping usually takes 3-5 business days within the US and 7-14 days internationally.

Can I cancel my order?

You can cancel your order within 24 hours of placing it.

Is there a minimum order requirement?

No, there is no minimum order requirement. You can purchase as little or as much as you need.

Do you offer gift wrapping services?

Yes, we offer gift wrapping services for an additional fee. You can select this option during checkout.

What is your return policy for damaged items?

If you receive a damaged item, please contact our customer service within 7 days of delivery for a replacement or refund.

How can I track my order?

You will receive a tracking number via email once your order has been shipped. You can use this number to track your order on our website or through the carrier's website.

What is your privacy policy?

Our privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information. You can review our privacy policy on the dedicated page available in the footer of our website.

Are your products eco-friendly?

Yes, we prioritize sustainability and eco-friendliness in our product selection. Check our product descriptions or the "About" page for more details on our commitment to environmental responsibility.

Can I change my shipping address after placing an order?

Unfortunately, we cannot change the shipping address once an order has been placed. Please double-check your information before confirming your purchase.

Do you have a loyalty program or rewards program?

Yes, we offer a loyalty program where you can earn points for every purchase. These points can be redeemed for discounts on future orders. Learn more about our loyalty program on the "About" or "FAQs" page.

What is your customer service response time?

Our customer service team strives to respond to inquiries within 24 hours. If you have urgent concerns, please use our priority contact methods listed on the "Contact" page.
